Information

Between You and Me works by finding the midpoint between two people. It will then suggest places in the area that match the activity you want to do.

Instructions:

  1. Pick the activity from dropdown menu that you want to do.
  2. Put your address in the first address field.
  3. Put the address of who you want to meet in the second address field
  4. Click the "Let's Go!" button to view the results
